## Rules and Mechanical Changes

- Looting Rule: After an encounter, a player looting a body rolls a percentile dice to determine loot quality. Contextual factors (e.g., defeating a dragon vs. goblins) influence the interpretation of the roll result.
- Awesome Point: Once per session, players can use an 'Awesome Point' to gain a +1 bonus on an attack roll after missing by 1, by describing a creative attack maneuver.
- Random Encounter Rule: When traveling, roll two dice of the same type. If both dice show the same number, a random encounter occurs. Dice type varies by context (e.g., 2d20 for safe areas, 2d4 for dangerous territories).
