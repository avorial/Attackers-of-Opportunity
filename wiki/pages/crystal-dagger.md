## Description

- Found by Dirt on tree; used as signal for raft pickup.
- A magical dagger that causes the wyrmling to recoil in fear.
- A dagger made of pure crystal, described as a 'talisman of power for good'. It grants +1d4 damage against dragons and can be used for divine smite.
- Stuck in the gut of the wounded dragon, causing blood to leak.
- Sticking from Valak's stomach; used to cut ropes.
- A weapon belonging to Maeve, which she is conflicted about retrieving from Valak.
- Found on a goblin's belt; sparks with electricity when touched.
