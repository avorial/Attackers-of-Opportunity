## Synopsis

- A goblin patrol consisting of three hub goblins, two bug bears, and two regular goblins is encountered at a crossroads. The hub goblins are directing the patrol, while the regular goblins are skirmishers.
- Maeve shoots the hobgoblin captain with an arrow, dealing 20 damage. The arrow hits the captain's shoulder despite his shield.
- Dickey transforms into a constrictor snake and attacks the hobgoblin captain. The transformation is triggered by a scream.
- Valak charges on Aubrey's back and attacks the hobgoblin captain with a greatsword, using divine smite and branding smite for 29 damage.
- Maeve casts Shatter, dealing 29 damage to the hobgoblin captain and one bugbear. The spell causes enemies to liquefy and bleed from ears/eyes.
- A sonic boom-like explosion from a Shatter spell takes out two foes, causing bones to crack and liquefy.
