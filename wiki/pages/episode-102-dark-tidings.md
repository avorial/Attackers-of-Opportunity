## Synopsis

- Players encounter a goblin patrol consisting of hobgoblins, bugbears, and goblins fighting with discipline and tactics.
- After defeating the patrol, players question a captured goblin named Grafon about Vazrul's forces and tactics.
- Grafon reveals Vazrul's warriors are receiving inferior weapons, and that hobgoblins and bugbears are favored by Vazrul.
- Grafon mentions Vazrul's forces include 5-6 giants, but the giants are now hostile to Vazrul after a conflict.
- Players gift Grafon a hobgoblin dirk (18-inch blade) as a replacement for his broken weapon.
- Players arrive at Warpen Village, finding evidence of a recent skirmish but minimal damage and ongoing goblin activity.
