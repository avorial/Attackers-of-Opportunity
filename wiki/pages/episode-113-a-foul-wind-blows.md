## Synopsis

- The party retrieves Wyvern eyes from a Wyvern graveyard in Kameli after observing the Wyvern life cycle involving a red gem and a heart-shaped object.
- The party is en route to Jasso, the capital of Zixa, when four air elementals appear near the river bordering Chamelee and Zixa, attempting to prevent them from crossing.
- Air elementals command wyverns to dive toward the ground, forcing the party to make Animal Handling checks (with advantage due to prior wyvern riding experience) to stay mounted.
- During the fall, air elementals create a whirlwind that flings wyverns sideways, requiring Athletics/Acrobatics checks to avoid being thrown off.
- Dorian and Maeve (substituting for Ellie) are flung off their wyverns but land safely due to Feather Fall magic.
- Combat begins with air elementals attacking the party. Moonbeam summons a wildfire spirit (Flame Seed) to attack an air elemental, dealing 7 fire damage.
