## Synopsis

- A group of adventurers discusses arranging an audience with a dragon named Olish, who is located on a turtle-shaped island.
- The group debates the feasibility of drinking dragon blood to gain powers, but clarifies that it's about bathing, not drinking.
- Dorian exits the tavern to retrieve bacon, causing the innkeeper and serving staff to calm down and return to their tasks.
- The group agrees to arrange a meeting with Olish, acknowledging the risks of being scryed upon or killed.
- Moonbeam, a Wyvern, volunteers to communicate with Olish, as she can transform and speak Wyvern, which may be understood by the dragon.
- A knowledge check (Arcana 22) reveals that Wyverns have beast-level intelligence and are considered lesser by dragons, similar to humans and apes.
