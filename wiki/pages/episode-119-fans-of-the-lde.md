## Synopsis

- The group is on the swift ship of Captain Black Fang after completing the Dragon Task, sailing west and north toward the coastal port of Knows.
- Captain Black Fang comments on the group's behavior, calling them 'special' and warning that if their secrets harm Maeve, he will take action.
- Dorian asserts that his secrets would protect Maeve, leading to a discussion about Maeve's past and a failed Insight check (rolled a 7) regarding her parents.
- Maeve confronts Dickey after he insinuates dark secrets about her, leading to a tense standoff and a warning from Captain Black Fang.
- The group arrives at Kudos Harbor, with the ship maneuvering smoothly into port. A harbor master named Thordid greets them and demands 25 gold for five days of stay.
- Maeve sneaks into port to avoid paying taxes, while the group uses a bag of 1,000 gold (200 gold each) provided by Captain Black Fang.
