## Synopsis

- The party arrives at the Granite Slab, a luxurious restaurant in the deep city, described as having enchanted crystals, fine dining, and a prestigious reputation.
- The party is greeted by a maitre d' and informed that they have a reservation under the name 'Little Dicky of Atten, the sausage king of Joseph.'
- The party is joined by three dwarves from the Silverthrust family, who are described as wearing gold-threaded clothing with silver symbols of a sword and hammer. They are introduced as Hernal (oldest), a young dwarf, and an elderly female dwarf.
- The Silverthrusts discuss their family's history of fighting the necromancer Lord Darragh and their current need to destroy an artifact to prevent his return. They agree to meet the party at their estate the next day.
- The party is shown to a private room for dinner. The bill is 800 gold, and Valak pays 1,000 gold with a tip.
