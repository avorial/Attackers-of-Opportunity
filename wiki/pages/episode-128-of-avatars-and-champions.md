## Synopsis

- A large magical beast (30 feet tall, 25 feet wide, three legs, three arms, and a gaping maw) is encountered in a cavern. It is described as having thick hide, magical energy radiating from it, and a heartbeat-like thrumming in the ground.
- Moonbeam casts Flame Strike on the creature's maw, dealing 31 damage (15 fire + 16 radiant). The creature becomes aware of the party and attacks.
- Valak attempts to communicate with the creature, which responds with a voice in his mind: 'Your death will be at the smashing of my foot.'
- Dorian casts Wall of Light (described as a 'Cinnamon Roll of Light') to block the creature's movement.
- The creature's third arm smacks the ground, causing a tremor that forces a Dexterity saving throw (DC 14).
- Moonbeam summons a fire spirit and deals 28 fire damage to the creature.
