## Synopsis

- The Citadel is described as a seven-story building resembling a large tree with four large windows, one on each side. The Council of Four (Spring, Summer, Winter, Fall) has chambers set up so that each council member faces their opposite.
- The party arrives at the Citadel's main entryway and sees four desks with elves in seasonal colors (Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter). They approach the Winter Court.
- Little Dickey of Atten from the Zixa Empire approaches the Winter Court elf Fallon, who schedules a meeting with the council in two weeks after a natural 20 roll and persuasion check.
- Fallon suggests contacting the military for faster access to the Winter Court. The party decides to investigate a reward posting about a woman with a terrifying visage at Lake Bryn.
- The party investigates the reward posting, learns the woman was sighted near Lake Bryn by Ryleth, a member of the Winter Court. They plan to visit the Lost Hills Tavern to gather more information.
- The party arrives at the Lost Hills Tavern in the Winter Court Quadrant, orders food, and interacts with Ryleth, who confirms the woman's sighting and shares details about the ghostly figure.
