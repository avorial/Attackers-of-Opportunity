## Synopsis

- The party is advised to cross the river either from the current location or from the opposite side of the goblin-controlled territories to avoid conflict with goblins.
- The river is described as fast-flowing, deep, and rocky, making it dangerous to cross without a ford, bridge, or other means.
- A shaman is consulted, revealing the existence of a wooden bridge spanning a gorge to the south, which may still be present.
- Maeve scouts south and discovers a grisly site with owl bear corpses, goblin weaponry, and signs of a past conflict between goblins and hobgoblins.
- Maeve finds broken goblin arrows, a shattered sword, and tracks indicating a large goblin patrol passed through the area.
- Valak attempts to cross the river using a rope, requiring three athletics checks with disadvantage (DC 18). He fails initially but succeeds on his third attempt.
