## Synopsis

- A group of adventurers (excluding Dorian) sees remnants of a Palisade-type wall with dead goblins and a small half-elf child (12 years old) near an elf in a green and brown cloak who is dead. The elf is lying face down in mud with dried blood, and four goblins are dead (three with arrows, one with a dirk).
- A medicine check (89) is made, reviving the elf who then realizes his son is dead and mourns. The elf is named Pelvernor, and his son was killed by goblins. The goblins were sent by a bugbear demanding tribute.
- Pelvernor is revealed to have been dishonorably discharged from the Bahia rangers 10 years ago. The group agrees to help him avenge his son's death and investigate the goblin threat.
- Pelvernor retrieves a heavy metal recurve bow with an emerald inlaid on the front sight post from a goblin's corpse. He also takes a black-bladed dagger and herbs from a crate in his home.
