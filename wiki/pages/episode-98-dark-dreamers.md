## Synopsis

- Dorian performs a detect magic ritual on the wall, which takes 10 minutes but fails to detect magic due to the stone blocking it.
- Velik investigates the wall, finding three stones that may be part of a secret door mechanism. The group attempts various combinations before discovering the correct sequence (first, third, second, first) after 15 minutes.
- The secret door opens to reveal a dark tunnel. Dorian uses his devil's sight to see the tunnel extends a few feet before turning left.
