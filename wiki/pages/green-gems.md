## Description

- Magical items that blend with the wearer's aura, potentially powered by an artifact or requiring recharging through rituals.
- Magical or valuable artifacts being tracked by the group. They are linked to a mind-warping influence in Seasons Glade and are needed for the wedding event.
- 327 green gems that are all activated. They weaken a person's mental resolve, giving disadvantage on wisdom or intelligence saving throws against mental attacks like dominate person.
- Embedded in rings and cloak pins; used for mind control via a ritual. Removing them breaks the mind control but leaves victims in recursive loops.

## Owner(s)

- Valak (wears one), others (need fake ones).
- Various NPCs
- Unknown; believed to be controlled by changelings.
- guards
