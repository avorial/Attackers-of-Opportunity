## Description

- An extremely long hallway running the length of the citadel, possibly ending over the water at the cliff above the spring. It is 105 feet long, with skeletons attached to the walls every 10 feet. The hallway jogs left 80 feet from the entrance and has another turn at the far end.
- A hallway with a noxious fume trap triggered by door movement.
