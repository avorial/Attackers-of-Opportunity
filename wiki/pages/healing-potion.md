## Description

- Used by Maeve to heal herself after being attacked. Provides 24 points of healing.
- Available for use on Love Dickey.
- Mentioned in a prior episode as a reward traded by Dirt
- Restores 13 hit points to Solana.
- Cures any disease affecting the user and removes blinded, deafened, paralyzed, and poisoned conditions.
- A full healing potion
- Consumed by a party member, providing minimal healing (4 hit points).
