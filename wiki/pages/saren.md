## Description

- A large trading hub and agricultural center on the shore of Lake Bryn, serving as a stopover point for northern towns and located on a major road leading into Seasons Glade. It is a walled city and the administrative and population center of the area, with a small garrison of soldiers and rangers. The town is known for its shrines, including one to Libelus Enerath, and has a cultural and administrative district called Elm Wall.
- A town where the party has spent time, featuring a tavern and areas with Thieves' Cant signals.
