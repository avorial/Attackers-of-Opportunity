## Synopsis

- Dinner at Chef Burl's facility. The food is described as bland, with the bread being the best part. Staff eat separately, with Dr. Aratus and Nurse Fisher chatting, and Director Madsen being stern with his assistant Olivas.
- Barris sets a trap, causing a crash and a cry for help from a fallen resident. This triggers the group's plan to head to the kitchen.
- Players sneak through the facility's hallways to reach the kitchen, avoiding orderlies with stealth checks.
- The kitchen is described as a medieval fantasy setting with a hearth, tables, and a locked back store room. The chef (Chef Burrow) has the key.
- Players attempt to pick the lock on the back store room using thieves tools. After three minutes of effort, they succeed.
- Inside the back store room, magical glyphs cool the room by converting heat from the hearth. The room contains ice cream containers (strawberry, chocolate, peanut butter) and whiskey barrels.
