## Synopsis

- The party is escorted to the tortle official's house, a three-story mansion with gold-plated furniture and high-quality woodwork.
- The party discusses plans to infiltrate the Turtle Clan castle, considering options like starting a fire as a distraction, sneaking in at night, or using a cat transformation for reconnaissance.
- A player (Broceus) transforms into a domestic short-haired tuxedo cat to scout the castle, passing stealth checks and gathering intel on guard positions.
- The cat scouts the castle, noting a group of 10 armored tortles on the third floor, three guards on the fourth floor, and a massive throne room on the fifth floor with a sword-themed throne.
- The cat is spotted by guards, leading to a chase, a failed escape attempt (falling from the fourth to third floor with 4 damage), and returning to the townhouse.
- The party uses persuasion checks to manipulate the tortle official, successfully gaining his cooperation through flattery.
