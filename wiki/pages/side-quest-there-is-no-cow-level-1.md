## Synopsis

- Jack sneaks up next to the barn and considers scaling it but decides against it due to potential attention.
- Jack successfully sneaks around the barn and is rewarded with 26 points for finding a cow by itself.
- Jack rolls a percentile dice and gets 33, which is considered 'not great' by the DM.
- Two groups of cows are observed: four milling about and two patrolling in a disciplined manner.
- Jack chooses to target the patrolling cows, believing they are closer to the problem.
- Jack throws a rock at a cow, hitting its shoulder with a Dex check of 14 (including proficiency bonus). The rock does not damage the cow.
