## Description

- A finely crafted stone structure within a mountain, three stories tall. The first floor has no windows, the second has narrow slitted windows, and the third has partial flooring with normal-looking windows. The tower has two roofs: one atop the third floor's partial structure and another covering the second floor. The interior is larger than the exterior, with pocket dimensions and an extra hidden floor.
